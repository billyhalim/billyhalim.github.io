SHORT BIOGRAPHY

Hello, my name is Billy Halim. I am 18 years old. I born on September 25, 1997 in Medan, North Sumatra, Indonesia. My nationality is Indonesian. Now, I live in Medan. I am the first child of 2 brothers and 1 sister in my family. I am an alumni of Sutomo 1, a school in Medan. Now, I am a Computer Science student at University of Sumatera Utara, Indonesia. I take Computer Science degree in University because I have been interested in Computer Science field especially Web Development and a little bit on Mobile Apps Development since the early of 2015. I currently focus on Front-End Web Development which I am responsible to create the user interface of a website. There is still no planning for me to move to Back-End Web Development. My goal is to become a Full-Stack Web Developer. I also like reading, writing, and get updated to stuff related to technology.
